Why is this happening?

The server uses a .ml domain (Mali's country-code TLD). Because .ml domains are frequently abused by phishing and malware sites, many antivirus products and ISPs — especially in Southeast Asia — automatically block or flag them regardless of the actual content.

This means you might see a browser error, a blank page, or an antivirus popup even though the site itself is completely safe.

SEA players: Installing Cloudflare WARP is the single fastest fix — it often resolves both the DNS blocking and disconnection issues in one step, for free.
