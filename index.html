What's going wrong?
Pick your problem.

There are two distinct issues players run into. They have different causes and different fixes — pick the one that matches what you're experiencing.

🚫
Problem 1
I can't reach the site at all
The forum or website fails to load — you see a browser error, a blank page, or your antivirus blocks it before it even opens.
Common causes: ISP DNS block Antivirus web shield Deep packet inspection
⚡
Problem 2
I keep disconnecting from the game
The site loads fine and you can log in, but you drop from the game server mid-session — lag spikes, timeouts, or random disconnects.
Common causes: Poor ISP routing Packet loss in transit ISP congestion

Why does the site get blocked?

The server uses a domain.

This is a reputation problem, not a content problem — the site is completely safe. The fix is either changing where your computer looks up domain names (DNS), telling your antivirus to allow it, or encrypting your traffic so your ISP can't inspect and block it.

Why do disconnects happen?

The game server is hosted in Montreal (OVH). Depending on where you are and who your ISP is, the network path between you and Montreal may be inefficient — packets take unnecessary hops, hit congested links, or experience high latency. This isn't something we can control on our end.

Cloudflare WARP can help by rerouting your traffic through Cloudflare's network, which often has a cleaner path to the server. It doesn't help everyone equally — if your existing route to Montreal is already good, WARP may actually make things slightly worse. It's worth testing.
